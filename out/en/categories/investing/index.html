Investing Learning Path

Investing is not chasing price moves; it is understanding assets, cash flow, risk, valuation, and compounding.

Suggested Order

  2. 2Understand companies, business models, and risk boundaries.
  3. 3Build your own watchlist instead of copying conclusions.
